## Important Notes

Random Forest is stronger than one Decision Tree because it combines many trees.

Bagging means each tree trains on a different random sample of rows.

OOB score is not bagging. OOB score is a validation method made possible by bagging.

Random Subspace Method means each tree/split uses only part of the features.

In this notebook, Random Forest worked best with 100 trees and max_features = 0.5.
